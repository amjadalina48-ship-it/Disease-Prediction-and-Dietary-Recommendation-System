About the Disease Prediction System

This project is a comprehensive health management tool designed to assist in the early identification of common ailments. By leveraging Machine Learning, the system processes patient symptoms—such as fever, fatigue, and body pain—to predict potential conditions ranging from the common cold to chronic issues like diabetes or hypertension.

🔍

Symptom Analysis

Input-driven diagnostic modeling based on 8 key clinical symptoms for rapid health assessment.

🥗

Clinical Nutrition

Intelligent mapping of over 15 specific diseases to evidence-based weekly dietary protocols.

📁

Historical Logging

Secure SQLite database integration to track patient progress and maintain long-term diagnostic history.

🛡️

Data Integrity

Built-in session management and encrypted data handling to ensure user privacy and research standards.

The ML Model

Utilizing a pre-trained classification model to provide real-time results with high precision.

Infrastructure

A scalable Flask-based architecture designed for low-latency performance in clinical environments.

Our Mission

To provide a data-driven foundation for proactive health management, empowering users with instant information and personalized recovery plans.
